About Tinchuley Gurung Homestay

Tinchuley Gurung Homestay offers a truly unique and authentic experience in the tranquil hamlet of Tinchuley. Just 30 kilometres away from the bustling town of Darjeeling, our homestay provides a peaceful escape from the crowds, surrounded by high pine forests and lush tea gardens.

Experience Warm Gurung Hospitality & Genuine Care

At Tinchuley Gurung Homestay, the heart of our hospitality is the warm embrace of the Gurung family. Every guest is treated like family, ensuring they feel not just welcomed, but truly at home.

Embrace Nature's & Connect with the Himalayas

Nature is our constant companion. With breathtaking sunrise views, serene village walks, and stargazing under the clear night sky, you'll find yourself immersed in the natural wonders of Tinchuley.

Savor Authentic Local Flavors Organic Home-Cooked Meals

Our meals are more than just sustenance; they are a taste of Darjeeling's culture. Prepared with locally sourced, organic ingredients, our home-cooked meals are a culinary journey that reflects the heart and soul of our region.

Immerse in Local Traditions with Authentic Cultural Experiences

Discover the heart and soul of a destination as you immerse yourself in local traditions through our authentic cultural experiences. Let the rich tapestry of heritage and customs come to life, making your journey a truly memorable and enriching one.

Ready to embark on a journey of serenity and natural beauty?
