About Tinchuley Gurung Homestay

Tinchuley Gurung Homestay offers a truly unique and authentic experience in the tranquil hamlet of Tinchuley. Just 30 kilometres away from the bustling town of Darjeeling, our homestay provides a peaceful escape from the crowds, surrounded by high pine forests and lush tea gardens.
