Farm-to-Table Dining

At Tinchuley Gurung Homestay, every meal is a celebration of local flavors. We take pride in serving wholesome, home-cooked food prepared with organic ingredients sourced directly from our own farm and local markets.

Organic & Locally Sourced

Fresh from the Farm

Our kitchen is fueled by nature. We grow our own seasonal vegetables like spinach, cauliflower, and beans without any chemicals. What we don't grow, we source fresh from the neighboring organic farms of Tinchuley.

  • 100% Organic
  • Farm Fresh
  • Seasonal Menu
  • Zero Preservatives

All-Day Meal Plan

Complete Culinary Care

Our standard package includes four wholesome meals a day to keep you energized for your adventures.

Morning: Bed Tea & Cookies
Breakfast: Puri Sabji / Tibetan Bread / Eggs
Lunch: Rice, Dal, Organic Sabji, Egg Curry/Fish
Dinner: Roti/Rice, Chicken Curry/Veg, Dessert
  • Veg & Non-Veg Options
  • Customizable Spice Levels
  • Traditional Recipes

Authentic Himalayan Flavors

Taste of Culture

Experience the true taste of the hills with our special traditional dishes. From savory Gundruk (fermented leafy greens) to steaming hot Momos and Sel Roti, our home-cooked meals offer a delightful culinary journey into Gurung culture.

  • Gundruk & Sinki
  • Homemade Pickles
  • Sel Roti (On Request)
  • Local Chicken
